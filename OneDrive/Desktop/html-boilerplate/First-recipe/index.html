Description

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic Indian rice dish made with basmati rice, tender meat (chicken, mutton, or beef), and a blend of spices. It's known for its rich taste and fragrant aroma, making it a festive favorite across many households.

Traditionally cooked by layering marinated meat and partially cooked rice, it is then slow-cooked to perfection. This recipe brings the classic taste of biryani right to your kitchen!
