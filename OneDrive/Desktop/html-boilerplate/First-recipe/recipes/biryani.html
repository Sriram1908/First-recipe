Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste
  • 2 tsp biryani masala powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh coriander (cilantro)
  • 1/4 cup mint leaves
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil or ghee

Steps

  1. Rinse the basmati rice until the water runs clear, then soak for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a large pot. Fry sliced onions until golden brown. Set aside half for garnishing.
  3. Add garlic and ginger paste, saut√© for 2 minutes.
  4. Add chicken pieces and cook until they start to brown.
  5. Mix in tomatoes, yogurt, biryani masala, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Cook until chicken is tender.
  6. Boil water separately, add soaked rice and cook until 70% done. Drain.
  7. Layer the cooked chicken and rice in the pot. Sprinkle chopped coriander, mint leaves, and reserved fried onions on top.
  8. Cover the pot tightly and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes (dum cooking).
  9. Turn off the heat and let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
  10. Fluff the rice gently and serve hot with raita or salad.
